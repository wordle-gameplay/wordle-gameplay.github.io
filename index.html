Wordle Game: Guess the Hidden Word

Guess the hidden word! You have six tries to find a secret word between 3 and 8 letters long. Start by typing any word. Letters that are correct and in the right spot turn green. Letters in the word but in the wrong place turn yellow. Letters not in the word stay gray. Can you solve it in 6 tries?
